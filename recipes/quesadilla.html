3 Cheese Quesadilla

Description

Make a simple and delicious, three-cheese quesadilla. In less than 10 minutes you can have a deliciously crispy quesadilla for breakfast or lunch! Follow this easy recipe with ingredients you can find at your local grocery store. All you will need is three types of cheeses, butter, and tortillas.

Ingredients

  • Butter(or olive oil)
  • Shredded cheese(oaxaca cheese,pepper jack cheese, and mozarella cheese)
  • Flour tortillas

Steps

  1. Add 2-3 tbsp to a large skillet or griddle over medium heat. Once butter starts to foam, add a tortilla onto the pan.
  2. Spread the cheeses in an even layer all throughout the tortilla
  3. Place another tortilla on top and cook for about 2-3 minutes until you have achieved a golden color
  4. Briefly remove tortilla and add another 2-3 tbsp to pan and cook the other side of the tortilla for 2-3 minutes
