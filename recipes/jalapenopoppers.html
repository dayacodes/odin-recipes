Jalapeño Poppers

Description

Spicy, creamy with a delicious bacon flavor! Cream cheese-stuffed jalapeños wrapped in bacon.

Ingredients

  • Jalapeños(as many as you would like)
  • Cream cheese
  • Bacon(or turkey bacon)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Wash jalapeños, remove the seeds and cut into halves.
  3. Fill jalapeños with cream cheese and wrap them up with bacon.
  4. Place jalapeños in baking pan and place in the oven.
  5. Cook for 20-25 minutes until bacon is crispy and brown.
