Queso Dip

Description

Spicy, creamy, and cheesy!Have a taste of this delicious velveeta cheese dip!

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of mexican salsa(La Coste√±a, Herdez, or similar brands).
  • 1 block of velveeta cheese.
  • Nacho chips or chips of your choice.

Steps

  1. Open the velveeta cheese block and cut up into 4 pieces.
  2. Open cans of mexican salsa.
  3. Place velveeta cheese and mexican salsa into a pot or crockpot.
  4. If using crockpot, set to low heat and cook until cheese has melted stirring occasionally.
  5. If usint a pot, cook in low-medium heat stirring occasionally until the cheese has melted.
  6. Once you have a thick consistency with the salsa and cheese mixed well serve and enjoy!
